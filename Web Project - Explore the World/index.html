Chania, Crete

Chania is a city located in West Crete and one of the most picturesque villages on the island. Its beautiful nature, lively harbor, historical landmarks, the market full of goods and people, the interesting museums and beaches around it make it the perfect resort. One of the most popular landmarks is the port's Venetian lighthouse, the Archeological and Naval Museum. One of the must-try is the local food, which is known as one of the most delicious in the world.

×

Bali, Indonesia

Bali is a tropical island in Indonesia. It is rich in culture, history, dance, sculpture and painting. Bali is considered one of the most beautiful places in the world because of its beautiful nature, beaches and tropical climate. With its more than thousands of temples, Bali is also known as the "Islands of the Thousand Temples."

×

Sydney, Australia

Sydney is the largest and oldest city in Australia and is located in New South Wales. There are many historic buildings in Sydney with the status of cultural monuments. Bonday Beach is a famous beach that has become the most popular surf spot around Sydney. The Surf Fun Run is held here every year, with its end point reaching the beach.

×

Hawaii, USA

Hawaii is one of the most exotic places in the world. Due to the presence of coral reefs, the waters of the beaches are transparent and always calm, and the beach is strewn with numerous palm trees and greenery, among which are many hotels and restaurants. This is why Hawaii is still one of the top destinations in the world.

×

Amalfi, Italy

Amalfi is one of the most fabulous places in Italy. In Amalfi you can explore a world of adventure, beauty and style, enjoying the sandy and rocky beaches, history and beautiful scenery. Amalfi can definitely be called must-see destination.

×

Nessebar, Bulgaria

Nessebar is a city located in southeastern Bulgaria. It is an ancient city with a rich history. The city was declared an architectural and archaeological reserve. The Old Town has preserved valuable historical monuments. The new part of the city is characterized by modern architecture, has a wide variety of accommodation, restaurants, discos. The city has wonderful views that should not be missed

×

Splitas, Croatia

Split is the second largest city in Croatia and is located on the coast of Dalmatia. There is a plenty of beautiful views of the Adriatic Sea and the Mediterranean spirit of the city. In Split you can see some of the most beautiful Roman monuments - the Palace of Diocletian is declared by UNESCO as a World Heritage Site.

×

Bolata, Bulgaria

Bolata is a small and beautiful bay on the Bulgarian Black Sea coast. Bolata is also part of the Most Beautiful Bay in the World club. Because the water is clear, the place is calm. Many species of fish are found in the bay. Nearby are quite rocky and underwater caves, where remains of ancient settlements and evidence of life dating back four hundred years BC have been found.

×

Costa Brava, Spain

Costa Brava is a great place to relax and have fun. The area is rich in tourist attractions, has an incredible culture and can enjoy a variety of culinary temptations, as well as discover the preserved traditions associated with many celebrations and holidays, because the Spaniards love the fun events.
